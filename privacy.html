Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2025

Hi there! This policy explains the very basic information the bot collects to function and what we do with it. By using Sekaimiru, you agree to this policy.

Please note that your bot data does NOT connect or interact with your "HATSUNE MIKU: COLORFUL STAGE!" data in any way.

Information Collected:

  • Your Discord user ID: This is a unique number provided by Discord and is used solely for saving your custom bot profiles and preferences.
  • Profile Information: If you explicitly use commands to set a custom bio or a team of cards, the bio and the IDs of the chosen cards are stored in connection with your Discord User ID.
  • Favorited Items (cards, songs, etc.): If you explicitly choose to favorite any item, the IDs of these items are stored in connection with your Discord User ID and are available to you at all times.
